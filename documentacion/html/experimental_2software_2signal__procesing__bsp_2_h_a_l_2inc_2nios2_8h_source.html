158/*
159 * Bit masks & offsets for Nios II control registers.
160 * The presence and size of a field is sometimes dependent on the Nios II
161 * configuration. Bit masks for every possible field and the maximum size of
162 * that field are defined.
163 *
164 * All bit-masks are expressed relative to the position
165 * of the data with a register. To read data that is LSB-
166 * aligned, the register read data should be masked, then
167 * right-shifted by the designated "OFST" macro value. The
168 * opposite should be used for register writes when starting
169 * with LSB-aligned data.
170 */
171
